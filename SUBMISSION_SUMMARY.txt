Top Fraud Indicators (SHAP-driven insights):

1. SIGNUP-TO-PURCHASE TIME < 10 SECONDS
   • Fraud rate: 99.8% (essentially deterministic)
   • SHAP importance: 28.7% of model decisions
   • Action: Real-time flag for 2FA/CAPTCHA verification
   • Expected fraud prevention: 5–10%

2. DEVICE SHARING (count ≥ 2)
   • Fraud rate: 20% vs 9.4% baseline
   • SHAP importance: 18.4% of model decisions
   • Action: Device fingerprinting + step-up authentication
   • Expected fraud prevention: 8–12%

3. IP SHARING (count ≥ 2)
   • Fraud rate: 15–20% in sharing cohorts
   • SHAP importance: 15.8% of model decisions
   • Action: Network analysis for fraud rings
   • Expected fraud prevention: 3–5%

4. GEOLOCATION (country = "Unknown")
   • Fraud rate: 15% vs 9.4% baseline
   • SHAP importance: 8.9% of model decisions
   • Action: Geographic friction tuning
   • Expected false positive reduction: 3–5%

5. USER AGE & ACQUISITION SOURCE
   • Younger users (< 25): 12% fraud vs 6% (age > 45)
   • Ads-acquired: 11% fraud vs 8% (Direct)
   • Action: Cohort-based thresholds
   • Expected improvement: +5–10% customer satisfaction
